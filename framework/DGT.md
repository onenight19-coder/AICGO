# DGT

## DaChun Growth Theory

```text
Growth Potential = Value x Entry x Propagation x Timing
```

DGT is a simple reasoning framework for judging whether a product or startup has real growth potential before large-scale investment.

DGT 是一套用于判断项目增长势能的推理框架，帮助创业者在大规模投入之前看清增长结构。

---

## Value

Why users stay.

用户为什么留下。

Questions:

- What core problem does the product solve?
- Is the product useful enough for repeated use?
- Does the value become clearer over time?

---

## Entry

Where users come from.

用户从哪里来。

Questions:

- What is the first distribution channel?
- Is the entry point natural and low-friction?
- Can the product enter an existing user behavior?

---

## Propagation

Why more users come.

用户为什么越来越多。

Questions:

- Does usage create sharing?
- Does every new user increase future reach?
- Is there a natural loop between value and distribution?

---

## Timing

Why now.

为什么是现在。

Questions:

- What changed in technology, market, behavior, or regulation?
- Why was this difficult before?
- Why is this opportunity open now?
