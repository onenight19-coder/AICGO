# Case 003: Kimi

## Kimi 的 AI 时代增长势能

### English

Kimi grew because it found a clear user value in the AI era: long-context reading, summarization, and reasoning.

Its value was concrete. Users could upload long documents, read complex materials, and ask questions across large amounts of text. This made AI feel useful for work and study, not only conversation.

Its entry point was strong because long documents are common in real scenarios: reports, papers, contracts, books, transcripts, and research materials.

Propagation came from visible usefulness. When users found that Kimi could handle documents other tools struggled with, they naturally shared screenshots, use cases, and workflows.

Timing was important. After ChatGPT educated the market, users were ready for AI assistants. The next question became: which AI tool is useful in specific work scenarios?

### 中文

Kimi 的增长来自 AI 时代一个非常清晰的用户价值：长文本阅读、总结和推理。

它的价值很具体。用户可以上传长文档，阅读复杂材料，并围绕大量文本提问。这让 AI 不只是聊天工具，而是工作和学习工具。

它的入口很强，因为长文档是真实场景里的高频需求：报告、论文、合同、书籍、会议记录、研究资料。

它的传播来自“看得见的有用”。当用户发现 Kimi 能处理其他工具不擅长的长文本任务时，很自然会分享截图、案例和工作流。

它的时机也很好。ChatGPT 已经完成了市场教育，用户开始寻找更具体、更适合工作场景的 AI 工具。

## Dominant Variable

Value.

Kimi 的核心增长变量是清晰、可感知、可复用的长文本处理价值。

## Growth Constraint

The product must keep improving model quality, reliability, cost efficiency, and workflow integration.

## Growth Potential

High.

Kimi has strong potential if it continues to turn long-context capability into repeatable work scenarios.
